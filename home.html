NEW BOOK: STORIES OF THE OLD TROLL


Buy on Amazon

Step into the enchanting Dark Forest and follow the escapades of the grumpy Old Troll in these original stories inspired by Scandinavian folk tales.

No matter how much his friend Olle, a little kind-hearted gnome, tries to keep him out of trouble, the next disaster is always waiting for the Old Troll just around the corner.

Like that time when he gets in a scrap with the scary Bog Witch and her minions, or runs into the vicious Troll Hunter, or when an ancient Mountain Bridge collapses under his feet... How does he manage to wiggle out? Does he learn anything from his trials? Well, pick up the book and find out what happens then!

COMING SOON: THE LITTLE WITCH OF SKOGGEVILLE

Elin doesn’t believe in magic—not after it failed to save her mother. Living in a quiet village near the shadowy Dark Forest, she wants nothing more than to protect what’s left of her family. But when her father falls ill and nothing else works, Elin sets off on a daring quest with a secret recipe for a mysterious potion.

Can she brave the dark woods? Can she survive the deadly swamp? And will she solve the ancient riddle before time runs out?

"The Little Witch of Skoggevile" is a tale woven with folklore and grit, following a girl who learns that sometimes, becoming what you fear is the only way to save what you love.
