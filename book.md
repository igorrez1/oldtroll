## Quick Summary
Step into the enchanting Dark Forest and follow the escapades of the grumpy Old Troll in these original stories inspired by Scandinavian folk tales.

No matter how much his friend Olle — a little kind-hearted gnome — tries to keep him out of trouble, the next disaster is always waiting just around the corner.

Like that time when he gets in a scrap with the scary Bog Witch and her minions, runs into the vicious Troll Hunter, or when an ancient Mountain Bridge collapses under his feet…

How does he manage to wiggle out? Does he learn anything from his trials? Pick up the book and find out!

## Why This Book Is Perfect for Ages 8–12
- Whimsical, light-hearted fantasy adventures rooted in Scandinavian folklore
- Funny mishaps and clever escapes that keep young readers engaged
- Positive friendship dynamic between the grumpy troll and helpful gnome
- Age-appropriate excitement — magical trouble without intense fear or violence
- Short stories format ideal for bedtime reading or independent readers

## Key Themes & Elements
- Friendship and loyalty (even when one friend is grumpy!)
- Bravery in the face of unexpected problems
- Learning (or not learning…) from mistakes — told with gentle humor
- Scandinavian-inspired creatures: trolls, gnomes, bog witches, and more
- Magical yet grounded world in the mysterious Dark Forest
